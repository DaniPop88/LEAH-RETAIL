Refund Policy

Our goal at Leahmae Camen Retail Mart is to provide quality products and a smooth shopping experience. If something isn’t right with your purchase, we’re here to help resolve the issue quickly and fairly.

Eligibility for Refunds & Returns

  • Refunds and returns are accepted for items that arrive damaged, defective, or incorrect.
  • Please notify us within 3 days of receiving your order to qualify for a return or refund.
  • Items must be in their original packaging, and you’ll need to present your official receipt.

How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact us at contact@leahmaecamen-mart.com or call +63 957 314 0289 within 3 days of receiving your item.
  2. Provide your order details, receipt, and photos of the issue if possible.
  3. Our team will review your request and guide you through the return or refund process.

What Happens Next?

  • Once your claim is approved, you’ll receive instructions on how to return the item (if needed).
  • Refunds are processed through your original payment method or via store credit, as you prefer.
  • Refunds may take up to 7 business days after approval, depending on your bank or payment provider.

Limitations

  • Refunds and returns are not available for items damaged due to misuse or normal wear and tear.
  • Some products (e.g., perishable goods, personal care items) may not be eligible for return due to hygiene or safety reasons.

Questions & Support

If you have any questions about our refund policy or need assistance, please reach out to our friendly team. We want you to feel confident shopping with us!
